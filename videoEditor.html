Video Editor

Iowa State University, English Department | September 2019 - Present

The Iowa State English department has 5 undergrad majors: English, English Ed, technical communication, linguistics, and speech communication. Graduates of these majors were filmed talking about how their major helped prepare them for grad school or life after college. The videos are on the English Department Website and the LAS college YouTube channel to promote the major to perspective students.
