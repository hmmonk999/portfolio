About Me

Currently

I'm Hannah Monk! I'm a graduate from Iowa State University (and "Zoom University" because of the 2020 Pandemic). I'm a multimedia specialist with experience in:

  • web design
  • document design
  • writing
  • video editing
My dream job is to be a user experience designer or a multimedia specialist. My favorite part about designing content is making it easily usable for all people! If UX doesn't work out, the job I've wanted since I was 2 is to be a pirate.

In College

I started off as an English Education major because I like writing and explaining difficult concepts, but quickly switched to technical communication. Technical communication combines audience analysis, writing, design, and computer science. I love the creative and analytical aspects of my major.

I learned all about time management and communication by having several jobs, taking an average of 17 credits, and having leadership roles in two clubs:

  • President of the Society for Technical Communication
  • Marketing Managers for the Web Development Club
I also learned to perfect my waffle making skills.

Growing Up

I grew up in a small town just outside of Champaign, Illinois.

Even when I was young, I enjoyed solving problems and explaining information in a way that best fits my audience. I honed this skill when I was a speaker at a University of Illinois engineering competition 3 years in a row. I had to speak differently to the engineering professors than I would to the five year olds with their grandparents. This is where my passion for audience analysis and rhetoric began.

I played travel softball for 10 years. Because of this, I gained teamwork skills and the power of being hard working.
